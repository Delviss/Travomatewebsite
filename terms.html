2. Description of Services

Travomate provides a peer-to-peer (P2P) parcel delivery platform through its App, connecting individuals who wish to send parcels ("Senders") with individuals who are already traveling to a specific destination and have available space to carry parcels ("Travellers"). The App facilitates:

  • Posting of parcel delivery requests by Senders.
  • Offering of parcel carrying services by Travellers.
  • Communication and negotiation between Senders and Travellers.
  • Secure in-app payment processing for delivery services.
  • Parcel tracking and delivery confirmation.
  • User verification, ratings, and review systems.
  • Optional insurance for parcels.

Travomate acts solely as an intermediary platform to connect Users and facilitate transactions. Travomate is not a postal operator, courier, or carrier, and does not take possession of parcels. The contract for the transport of the parcel is directly between the Sender and the Traveller.
