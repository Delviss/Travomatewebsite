FAQ

Quick answers to common questions

How does Travomate Parcels actually work?+

You post your parcel in the app. Verified travellers already heading to your destination send offers. You pick one based on rating, price, and timing, pay securely, and track the delivery — all inside the app.

Is it safe to send parcels with strangers?+

Every user is identity-verified, and you can see ratings and delivery history before you accept any offer. Payments stay locked in the app and are only released after successful delivery.

When does the traveller actually get paid?+

Funds are held securely from the moment you pay. They're released to the traveller only after you confirm the parcel has been delivered.

Is my parcel insured?+

Insurance is optional. You can add coverage at checkout to protect your shipment for extra peace of mind.

What about customs and legal stuff?+

The app guides you through shipping rules so every delivery follows customs and legal requirements. Restricted items are flagged before you post.

Can I earn money as a traveller?+

Yes. If you're already travelling, list your trip and accept parcels along the way. Turn your unused luggage space into income.
