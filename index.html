FAQ

Quick answers to common questions

How fast is same-day delivery?+

Most city pickups happen within 30 minutes of booking, and arrive at the recipient within 2–4 hours depending on traffic and distance.

Are international parcels insured?+

Yes. Every Travomate parcel is insured up to its declared value, with a default cover of $2,000 on the International plan.

How do you verify travelers?+

Every traveler completes government-ID verification, a selfie liveness check, and is rated by senders after each delivery.

Do you support API integration?+

Absolutely. Our REST API and Shopify/Woo plugins let you create shipments, fetch live tracking, and trigger webhooks on every status change.

What if my parcel is delayed or lost?+

You'll get proactive notifications, and our 24/7 support team will resolve or refund according to our service-level guarantee.
